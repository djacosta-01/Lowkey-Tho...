How to play: You will see a card with a prompt in the center of your screen. Then you answer the prompt with the blank card in the bottom of your screen by selecting it and typing a creative and hilarious response. Once everyone has submitted their cards or time has run out, you can vote for the card that you thought had the funniest response (You can't vote for yourself). The person with the most votes wins the round. The person with most votes wins the round. Rinse and repeat.
